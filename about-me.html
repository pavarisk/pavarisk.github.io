A Little bit about me

My name is Pavaris Kakanat. As a Thai, it is customary that everyone has a nickname and I am no exception so my friends and family call me “Bomb”. I was born in Bangkok, Thailand but I was raised in a town located in South Thailand called "Nakhon Si Thammarat". I lived there for the first 13 years of my life and moved to New Zealand in late 2008. It was one of the most fun AND scary experiences in my life. It was fun because it was a journey to a completely new country where everything was different, especially the culture and the food. Now about the scary part. I found it very intimidating when I was moving over here because I did not know how to verbally communicate in English. I only knew a few words and how to read simple sentences. I cannot say that it was an easy journey but I can say it was very fun.

My past job experiences have mostly been in various Customer Service roles. I have also worked as an Inventory Controller Team member in a wine production and storage warehouse. The most prideful yet short lived career I have had was as a Flight instructor but due to the global pandemic, the industry took a major hit and will take some time to recover. This means that the pilots will be losing flying jobs until further notice. However, I am glad that I have found coding. Learning to code has been loads of fun so far barring the stress induced, hair pulling moments when I cannot figure something out. However, I am hoping that once I look back on all of this, it will make a beautiful journey full of fun.
